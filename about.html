About

Learn more about one of the most popular South Korean Group produced by
SM Entertainment. This site provides an easy understanding of the group's
system. Neo Culture Technology site was created in the beginning of 2020
as a fan site, with the goal of bringing comprehensive and complete profiles
of the members of NCT.



The Story of Kristel

On the year 2013, Kristel discovered that she easily learned Photoshop
CS3 with the help of her teacher back then. As the years went by, she
improved a lot in terms of editing and making graphic designs. 2018, she
decided to pursue Information Technology because of how she's comfortable
and confident designing and creating digital stuffs.

Kristel pursued the passion of creating websites that would engage teens
on visiting her works as she makes websites that targets large fans around
the world. She herself is also a big fan of Korean Pop groups and she enjoys
designing and developing her idols and that inspired her to do more and
improve more.
