The Story of Kristel

On the year 2013, Kristel discovered that she easily learned Photoshop CS3 with the help of her teacher back then. As the years went by, she improved a lot in terms of editing and making graphic designs. 2018, she decided to pursue Information Technology because of how she's comfortable and confident designing and creating digital stuffs.

Kristel pursued the passion of creating websites that would engage teens on visiting her works as she makes websites that targets large fans around the world. She herself is also a big fan of Korean Pop groups and she enjoys designing and developing her idols and that inspired her to do more and improve more.
